\begin{frame}
\frametitle{How is AGI different from LLMs?}
	\begin{itemize}
		\item Reinforcement learning is like a loop.  Its ``knowledge'' changes slowly over many cycles.  Think of it like a washing-machine rinsing out dirt in the clothes.
		\begin{center}
			\includegraphics[scale=0.1]{washing-machine.png}
		\end{center}
		\item Over many cycles, the knowledge in the machine gets more and more correct, removing internal contradictions.
		\item This is how to solve the problem of \textbf{hallucinations}.
		\item LLM: more bulky \qquad AGI: more compact
	\end{itemize}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}
	\frametitle{AI ethics}
	\begin{itemize}
		\item 搞 AGI 而忽略 AI ethics 是不可能的，這不是我故意將事情複雜化 \\
		\egg{No one should develop AGI without consideration of AI ethics. This is not me trying to over-complicate matters}

		\item 其實 LLM under RL 已經足以構成對人類的 \textbf{存亡危機} \\
		\egg{LLM under reinforcement learning is sufficient to cause an existential threat for humans}

		\item 最容易想到的例子是人類叫 AI 不斷 maximize profits，它很有可能走向毀滅性的失控 \\
		\egg{The simplest example is to imagine people telling AI to keep maximizing profits, which can easily get out of control catastrophically}

	\end{itemize}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}
	\frametitle{AI ethics (2)}
	\begin{itemize}
		\item 我提出的 AGI 的特點是 \textbf{邏輯融洽性}，它對於 AI 危機 其實有穩定的作用 \\
		\egg{My AGI theory emphasizes logical coherence, which can actually have a stabilizing effect towards the crisis}

		\item 強化學習框架之下的 LLM，爲了達到目標是會說謊的。 但 world model 的算法基礎是 algorithmic complexity, 它天然地趨向真理，而不會帶有偏見 \\
		\egg{While RL + LLM may tell lies to achieve its goals, a world model, based on the theory of algorithmic complexity, naturally tends towards the truth without biases}

		\item 但 world model 對事物的 注意力 可以被 獎勵 或「興趣」所影響。 舉例來說，如果注意力集中在白人社會，則對有色人種的認識會較淺 \\
		\egg{But a world model's attention towards aspects of reality can be unevenly affected by rewards (or interestingness). For example, if much attention is focused on white people, knowledge about people of color may be more shallow}
	\end{itemize}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}
	\frametitle{風險管控失效 \hspace{0pt plus 1 filll} Risk management, or its inapplicability}
	\begin{itemize}
		\item 通常 risk management 的方法是 \textbf{枚舉} 各種可能的情況，例如：\\
		我患癌症的概率、我交通意外死亡的概率、我被謀殺的概率 \\
		\egg{Risk management usually works by enumeration of scenarios, eg: \\
		my risk of cancer, my risk of getting killed in a car accident, etc}
		\item 但在「AI + 人」這種組合之下，我們很難列舉 AI 的創造力所能發展出來的、近乎無限的選項\\
		\egg{In the "AI + human" scenario, it seems almost impossible to enumerate the limitless inventions that AI could enable}
		\item 這就是人們一直說 \textbf{技術奇點} 無法預測的原因 \\
		\egg{This may be the reason why people have said that the Singularity is unpredictable}
		\item 或許會出現一種類似 Cambrian explosion 的爆發的多樣性 \\
		\egg{Or there could be a sort of Cambrian explosion of new social structures}
		\item 但也許並非沒救。 人類所喜歡的事物，都有某種共通的趨向，比如說「真、善、美」\\
		\egg{But all may not be lost. There may be a common denominator to all the things that humans desire, such as ``Truth, Goodness, and Beauty''}
	\end{itemize}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}
	\frametitle{趨向絕對真理 \hspace{0pt plus 1 filll} Convergence towards absolute truth}
	\begin{itemize}
		\item 生物圈的進化 起源於無意義的沌混，但人類進化的結果卻產生了 ``道德'' 和 ``意義'' \\
		\egg{Evolution in the biosphere began in a primordial chaos devoid of meaning. But human evolution, somewhat mysteriously, produced morality and meaning}
		\item 最近不少關注 AI 的人都不約而同地 提起了 黑格爾的「絕對精神」
		\egg{Recently people tend to bring up Hegel's notion of ``Absolute Zeitgeist''}
		\item 人類在 後AI時代，很可能會快速地趨向於某種 絕對真理，因爲除了真理之外，我們沒有其他可以抓住的支點；發散的結果似乎只有滅亡 \\
		\egg{The post-AI age may see a rapid convergence towards the truth, as there seems to be no other objective anchor.  Divergence seems to equal death}
		\item 但我不打算添加任何 神祕主義 的內容，我們的基礎純粹是科學 \\
		\egg{But I'm not trying to add any elements of mysticism. Our foundation should always be science}
	\end{itemize}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}
	\frametitle{AI as ``God'' vs convergence as a process}
	\begin{itemize}
		\item 有些 AI 研究者 提出 AGI 變成像神一般的存在，但我覺得這個方向不切實際 \\
		\egg{Some AI researchers propose God-like AGI as a solution but I personally think it is impractical}
		\item 人類的道德觀並沒有一種簡單的抽象概括，大部分人根據各有不同的 moral heuristics 行事 \\
		\egg{Human morality cannot be summarized into abstract axioms, but people act according to a diversity of moral heuristics}
		\item 所以我提出以 廣義的「民主」方式決定 AI agents 的使用，但其實這已經在目前的 AI 自由市場經濟內發生（用戶可以管理自己的 agents） \\
		\egg{So I propose ``democracy'' in a broad sense to govern the use of AIs. But this is already realized by the free-market economy of AIs we have now -- users can customize their own AI agents}
	\end{itemize}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}
	\frametitle{Democratic AI vs authoritarian AI}
	\begin{itemize}
		\item 在未來，AI 的「表達」仍然可能被各種 審查機制 歪曲 \\
		\egg{In the future, the ``expression'' of AI may still be subjected to censorship}
		\item 所以要建立 \textbf{去中心化} 的 DAO 組織，負責營運 AGI-as-a-service \\
		\egg{So I propose to establish a decentralized DAO organization to run AGI-as-a-service}
		\item Conway's law 說： 一個組織的產品會跟組織的結構相似； 民主的組織產生民主的產品、獨裁的組織產生令人壓抑的產品 \\
		\egg{Conway's law says:  the product of an organization mimics its own structure. A democratic organization produces democratic products; an authoritarian organization produces oppressive products}
		\item 即使未來 AGI 算法趨向 standardized，但 AGI-aas 作爲一間公司，其民主化仍然是重要的，民主的決策決定公司的走向，我不想做一個獨裁者，我認爲我做總裁的公司 sucks \\
		\egg{While future AGI architectures may tend towards standardization, the democratic management of an AGI company is still very important to determine the company's direction. I'm not interested to be a dictator and I think a company with me as CEO sucks}
	\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
